Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of stacked layers of pasta alternated with fillings such as ragù (a meat sauce), vegetables, cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmigiano-Reggiano), and various seasonings and spices. The dish is baked in an oven, often resulting in a crispy top layer of cheese and a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450 grams) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces/410 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces/170 grams) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces/425 grams) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil (or 1 teaspoon dried basil)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano (or 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 container (15 ounces/425 grams) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until they are translucent.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Add ground beef (and ground pork if using) to the skillet. Cook until the meat is browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Drain excess fat from the skillet.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and red wine (if using).
  6. Add chopped basil, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  8. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente.
  9. Drain the noodles and lay them flat on a sheet of parchment paper or a clean kitchen towel to prevent them from sticking together.
  10. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  11. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x9 inch (23x23 cm) baking dish.
  12. Place 2 lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping them.
  13. Spread one-third of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  14. Spread one-third of the meat sauce over the cheese layer.
  15. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  16. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese over the top layer of meat sauce.
  17. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  18. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese on top is melted and bubbly.
  19. Let the lasagna cool for about 10 minutes before serving.
