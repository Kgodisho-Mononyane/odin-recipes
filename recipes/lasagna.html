Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of stacked layers of pasta alternated with fillings such as rag√π (a meat sauce), vegetables, cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmigiano-Reggiano), and various seasonings and spices. The dish is baked in an oven, often resulting in a crispy top layer of cheese and a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450 grams) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces/410 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces/170 grams) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces/425 grams) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil (or 1 teaspoon dried basil)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano (or 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 container (15 ounces/425 grams) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)
